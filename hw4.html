Tree of Sound

Have some fun pretending you're a piano virtuoso using machine learning!

Use the mouse numbered keys on your keyboard the home row the coloured blocks to play the piano. Use the space bar to control the sustain pedal. The more you pretend you're a real player, the better the melody (and you!) will sound.

Built with magenta.js, generative-gestaltung, tone.js and ,paper.js.
